Instructions for the game:

The goal of MemoryMaze is to test and improve your memory skills by matching pairs of images within a specified time limit and move count.


1.Choose a difficulty level: easy, medium, or difficult.
2.You have a specific time limit to complete each level.
3.In the easy level, find all pairs within the time limit is 2minutes and 13 moves.
4.In the medium , locate matching pairs within the time limit is 3minutes and 23 moves.
5. In the difficult level,locate matching pairs within the time you have is 10mintues and 45 moves.
